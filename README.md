# Combustion Inc Analyzer

A native Apple app for analyzing cook data from Combustion Inc wireless thermometers. Track, review, and share your cooks across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

## Features

- **CSV Import** — Load CSV exports from the official Combustion Inc app and explore your cook data in detail.
- **Cook History** — Browse past sessions, compare cooks, and never forget what worked.
- **Custom Notes** — Annotate each cook with personal notes to plan and refine your technique.
- **Sensor Deep Dive** — View temperature data from every individual sensor on the probe.
- **Live Monitoring** — Connect to your Combustion Inc probe via Bluetooth and watch temperatures in real time.
- **Share** — Export and share temperature graphs with friends and family.
- **Cross-Platform** — Runs natively on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
- **Free & Open Source** — Always.

## Compatibility

- iOS 16.0+
- iPadOS 16.0+
- macOS 13.0+ (Intel or Apple Silicon)

## Getting Started

1. Clone the repo
2. Open `CombAnalyser.xcodeproj` in Xcode 15+
3. Build and run
